Senior Design Team

Computer Science

The computer science team focused on designing a dashboard to remotely monitor live hydroponic data through a web-application. Ensuring seamless communication between Rockwell Automation's Connected Component Workbench software system and the cloud database server. The team's responsibilities also included the design, development, and configuration of the cloud database, as well as guaranteeing the accurate sending, receiving, and retrieval of data. Utilizing the MQTT protocol, the team emphasized UI/UX, implemented API protocols, and developed a full-stack web page with a user-friendly design. In addition, they contributed to the designing and creation of the Senior Design project webpage.

The computer engineering team members were responsible for programming the Rockwell Automation Micro800 PLC, configuring the microcontrollers, and programming the sensors and actuators within the hydroponic system. Mechanical Engineering

The mechanical engineering team members were responsible for designing the physical structure of the hydroponic system, selecting appropriate materials, and conducting stress and fluid dynamic analyses. They collaborated with the computer engineering and computer science teams to integrate the hardware and software components into the system.
